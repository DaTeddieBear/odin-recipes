Pulled Pork

Slow cooked pulled pork w/ coleslaw. Nice & Easy!

Ingredients (Qty.)

  • Pork Tenderloin
  • BBQ Sauce
  • Coleslaw
  • Greek Yogurt

Instructions

  1. Place Tenderloin in crock pot and cover with BBQ Sauce
  2. Cook on low for 8 hours
  3. Make coleslaw
  4. Shred tenderloin and place on Martin roll topped with coleslaw
